GENERAL SKILLED MIGRATION

This is a Permanent Residence program category which is based on Points based system. To apply under this category your occupation must come under Medium and Long-Term Strategic Skill List (MLTSSL) which was formerly Skilled Occupation List.

Under this category, applicant requires no sponsorship from any employer or a family member. No nomination by Australian State or Territory government is necessary.

  • Can live, work and Study in any part of Australia.
  • Eligible for health care benefits.
  • Free education and allowances for kids.
  • Can apply for Australian Citizenship right after 4 years.
  • Can sponsor their family members for Permanent Residence Visa.
  • Spouse/kids can also become part of the application
